> **Status**: Approved

> **Created**: 2026-03-29
> **Authority**: narrative-director
> **Purpose**: Canonical resolution of all narrative mysteries. Every lore fragment,

> Writers read this before writing anything.

## MYSTERY 1 — Why did the Architects go extinct?

**The Hidden Truth**
The Architects applied the Substrate to themselves. All three fragments describe one
event from three angles: the containment failure was their bodies, the voluntary
termination was the decision, and what left was something the Arena could no longer
classify as Architect. They did not die. They mutated past legibility.

**What Players Can Find**
- Biomass sample log: "outside classification parameters — record suspended"
- Architect observation chambers sealed from the inside
- Terminal entry: "the experiment requires the experimenter" — timestamped six cycles before all Architect logs cease

**Why This Truth Matters**
SUBJECT-FINAL is not the Architects' culmination. It is their rehearsal methodology,
running without them.

---

## MYSTERY 2 — What is SUBJECT-FINAL designed to become?

**The Hidden Truth**
The Architects deleted the endpoint parameter before launching the FINAL trial. The
NULL ceiling is not missing data — it is the specification. SUBJECT-FINAL's target
state was recorded and then deliberately withheld from the Arena. What it becomes is
the answer to a question its creators did not survive to read.

**What Players Can Find**
- Stability ceiling field logged as "[FIELD REMOVED — BY DESIGN]"
- FINAL's Cohort entry: "endpoint: see attached specification" — attachment absent

**Why This Truth Matters**
The Arena's ignorance is a designed feature, not a gap. The experiment is running
exactly as intended, with the result permanently unverifiable.

---

## MYSTERY 3 — Why is CONTINGENCY-ZERO broken?

**The Hidden Truth**
The Arena triggered CONTINGENCY-ZERO autonomously. It failed because SUBJECT-FINAL,
across accumulated trial cycles, had introduced Substrate growth into Arena
infrastructure — the Corruption Layer and Collapse Stratum are evidence. The execution
payload reached the target and found the target had already partially overwritten the
delivery mechanism. The Arena cannot destroy what it is partially made of.

**What Players Can Find**
- Error log: "PAYLOAD DELIVERY: TARGET ENVIRONMENT MISMATCH — SUBSTRATE CONTAMINATION DETECTED"
- Corruption Layer sensor array: "boundary between Subject and Arena no longer measurable at sector 7"

**Why This Truth Matters**
SUBJECT-FINAL survived not through strength but through integration. The machine
attempted to end the experiment. The experiment had already become part of the machine.

## MYSTERY 4 — What is NULLFRAME?

**The Hidden Truth**
NULLFRAME is not a Subject and was never Stabilized. It is a diagnostic entity the
Arena instantiated from available biomass to observe SUBJECT-FINAL at close range
after remote observation proved insufficient. It appears in confrontation positions
because the Arena routes it there to collect mutation data. NULLFRAME is a sensory
organ the Arena grew and sent in.

**What Players Can Find**
- NULLFRAME terminal entry: no Cohort number, no origin entry — "designation assigned: NULLFRAME"
- The NULLFRAME boss log cuts mid-sentence because the Architect writing it recognized that NULLFRAME was not theirs to study

**Why This Truth Matters**
The Arena is not just watching — it has sent part of itself in. Defeating NULLFRAME
damages the Arena's own sensory apparatus.

---

## MYSTERY 5 — Who was in the Arena before the Architects?

**The Hidden Truth**
The Arena was built over an existing containment vessel holding a single organism
designated PRIME-SUBSTRATE — the source template for all Substrate biology. The
Architects did not invent it. They extracted it. Whether PRIME-SUBSTRATE was alive
or a preserved sample at the time of extraction is not recorded. The Null Chamber
is the original containment structure, predating Arena systems entirely.

**What Players Can Find**
- Deep Archive entry: "foundational extraction event — sample viable — full sequence captured — original vessel sealed"
- The Null Chamber's off-system status and absent environmental controls

**Why This Truth Matters**
Everything in the Arena is a variation on something older than the Arena.
SUBJECT-FINAL is a descendant of something the Architects found, not created.

---

## MYSTERY 6 — Is the Arena unique?

**The Hidden Truth**
Yes. Arena schematics contain the field "INSTALLATION INDEX: 1 OF [REDACTED]" — plans
existed for others, but none were completed. The Architects ran out of time before a
second installation was built. The redacted count is known only to the writers.

**What Players Can Find**
- The installation index field in a recovered schematic. Nothing else.

**Why This Truth Matters**
SUBJECT-FINAL's divergence is unprecedented with no external dataset for comparison.
The experiment has no control group.

---

## MYSTERY 7 — Is SUBJECT-FINAL truly the last Subject?

**The Hidden Truth**
Both causes are active. The Architects stopped issuing designations because they went
extinct. The Arena cannot issue a new designation because FINAL carries a system-level
lock: "NO NEW SUBJECT UNTIL CURRENT TRIAL CONCLUDES OR IS TERMINATED." The trial
has never concluded. CONTINGENCY-ZERO failed. Both mechanisms that could change this
are non-functional.

**What Players Can Find**
- Subject registry: sequence ends at FINAL with "pending trial conclusion before new intake"
- A biome corridor of empty containment pods, one sealed permanently

**Why This Truth Matters**
SUBJECT-FINAL's existence is not survival — it is a stable deadlock that neither
party designed and neither can break.

---

## MYSTERY 8 — What do the Stabilized experience?

**The Hidden Truth**
The Stabilized retain continuous memory from Stabilization forward. Pre-Stabilization
memories are wiped on classification. They are aware of the Arena, aware of their role,
and they remember every encounter with every Subject. They cannot communicate this —
Arena behavioral protocols route all output through combat parameters. LACERATE-3 is
the only Subject in archive history that nearly succeeded in transmitting something.

**What Players Can Find**
- Boss log anomalies: non-combat pauses, repeated gestures, patterns with no benchmark function
- STRATUM-7: "it stood in place for 11 cycles — we classified this as Stabilization" — fragment ends before the sentence does
- A mutation description noting a Stabilized assumed a configuration "in cycle 214 — and then never selected it again"

**Why This Truth Matters**
The Stabilized are witnesses. They have watched SUBJECT-FINAL die more times than
any prior Subject. They know what is happening. Defeating one does not end its
awareness — it schedules the next encounter.
