# SYNTHBORN — Narrative Brief

> **Status**: Approved
> **Created**: 2026-03-28
> **Authority**: narrative-director

---

## Core Narrative Question
"What am I, and why does the Arena want me to become something else?"

## Emotional Arc

| Phase | Runs | Player Feeling | Lore State |
|-------|------|---------------|------------|
| Arrival | 1-5 | Disorientation, wonder | Arena feels alien, no context |
| Habituation | 6-15 | Mastery, experimentation | First boss fragments — hints of previous subjects |
| Unease | 16-30 | "Is this a trap?" | Mid-tier boss lore reveals the Architects existed |
| Suspicion | 31-45 | "I am being shaped" | Advanced lore: previous subjects, Arena's true function |
| Revelation | 46-55 | "I am what they built toward" | Final boss lore completes the picture |
| Completion | 55+ | Acceptance or resistance | Kill-switch fragment — player interprets |

## Lore Delivery Vehicles

1. **Boss Defeat Fragments** (Primary) — 3-5 sentence Architect data logs, clinical tone
2. **Arena Environment** (Secondary) — Art direction prompts per biome, no text required
3. **Mutation Names/Descriptions** (Tertiary) — Lore-carrying nomenclature
4. **Signal Archive** (Meta) — Collection screen for unlocked fragments

## Player Character: SUBJECT-FINAL

- No name, no voice, no internal monologue
- Communicates through physical transformation
- Does not resist mutation — seeks it
- Death = Arena reset (diegetic, canon)
- Pronouns in lore: "it/its" (clinical)

## Boss Philosophy: Mirrors

- Each boss = a mutation path taken to terminal extreme
- Bosses are Stabilized Subjects (stopped evolving)
- Not villains — stuck. Cautionary tales.
- Each boss lore fragment reveals: what path they took + why they stopped + what this tells us about the Arena

## Tone Pillars

1. **Clinical Curiosity** — Scientist field notes. Detached. Precise. (SCP Foundation)
2. **Incomplete Information** — Every fragment leaves questions. (Dark Souls)
3. **Body as Language** — Physical form tells everything. Metaphors from anatomy and engineering. (Cronenberg)

## What This Game Is NOT (Narrative Anti-Pillars)

- No heroic framing
- No exposition dumps
- No emotional manipulation
- No retcons — establish rules once, hold to them
